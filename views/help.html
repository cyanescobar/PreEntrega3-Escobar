How can we help?

Can I get a refund to my Zeus card?

You can receive refunds directly to your card even if the card has been deleted or cancelled. Please bear in mind that a refund can take between 7 and 15 days after the merchant approves the refund to appear in your account.

Who can help me if I have been hacked?

If you notice an unrecognised transaction on your account, please chat to us, you will be directed to the relevant, compliance team that will handle your case. If you've received a suspicious SMS or email, please fill out the form below to report it.
